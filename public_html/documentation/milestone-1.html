Andrei`s Personal Website Project

Purpose: In my Personal Website Project, I would like to emphasize a brief review of my skills to get attention of the potential employer.

Audience: It will be anyone who wants to learn about me. This is an website about Andrei, in a nicer way presented than a facebook or linkedIn page. The main targets are the employers which look to hire an Web-developer.

Goal:To make an amazing presentation about me, my skills, my knowledge and to get a full-time job.

  1. Name: Carlos Ross
  2. Gender: Male
  3. Age: 37 years
  4. Profession: Carlos owns a local IT company in Albuquerque which makes websites and internet contents. He sees demand now with Covid-19, and wants to act fast and to boost the company size by hiring new web-developers. He is looking to hire one or two persons which have direct hands-on knowledge as web-developers. But in the same time, he prefers to hire somebody who can learn and adapt quickly to the company way of codding and procedures.
  5. Technology:
    • Device: Pc office station: Windows 10, Chrome Browser
    • Device: Laptop home: Windows 10, Chrome Browser
    • Proficiency: Bachelor in Computer Science and large experience in Informational Technology field.
  6. Attitudes and Behaviors:
    • Carlos worked 8 years as a Senior Project Manager and now he is excited to work for himself.
    • He likes his role in the company and the environment which he creates for his team members.
    • He is a friendly and a nice person, who wants results from his team.
    • He likes to give a chance for juniors developers. It reminds him when he started, how hard and confusing was to find a job.
  7. Needs:
    • Carlos wants to add more staff to increase the production and quality of the content.
    • Due to Covid-19, he sees a lot of demand for his services, especially carry-out websites. Now he sees, that he has to act and find the best candidates.
    • The minim requirements for his positions are: JavaScript, React, HTML, CSS, Git and MySQL.
    • Carlos checks some candidates portfolios and this is how he finds Andrei`s website.
    • He is looking for a website with easy user content, classic-modern style which comes with nice colors, formats, responsive with good presentation of skills.
    • He sends a request for job interview, after Carlos checks all Andrei`s website features, .

User Story:Carlos as a Casual user, wants to get an impression of Andrei`s skills and level of his knowledge.
